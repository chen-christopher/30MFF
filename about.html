About

This film was shot and edited within thirty minutes in preparation for the 30 Minute Film Festival (30MFF) hosted by the NYU Abu Dhabi Interactive Media Department.

Pass It On is a short film that uses Zoom to showcase the interactions and experiences users can share through a screen. Despite COVID-19 deeply affecting all of us, we can still find joy and meaning in everyday events. Even the smallest things can leave lasting impressions.

This film celebrates both our differences and our similarities. With Pass It On, we aim to highlight how we can come together to create even when physically apart.
